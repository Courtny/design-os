# Stories that Impress

*Recipe — Nail that presentation!*

**Present your ideas with confidence. Don't bury them under dusty layers of business jargon.**

Hook our attention, make your facts flow like a story and master the art of show and tell. You've got an important message: say it with style!

## Back of Card

*Stories that Impress*

1. **Movie Time** — Make sure you're actually telling a story, not just giving facts and opinions
2. **Five Ts** — Get the beats of your story right with a simple structure.
3. **Story Hooks** — Grab your audience's attention from start to finish.
4. **Show and Tell** — Don't let your visual presentation bore or confuse people.
5. **Cut to the Chase** — Have a plan in case your presentation falls flat.
