## Back of Card

*Pick a card... any card*

...as a magician would say. Let's do some story magic! Here are a few ways to approach the Storyteller Tactics deck:

**a. "Pick a card: any card"**

1. Fan the deck, pick a card at random.
2. Make the elements of your story fit the tactic.
3. Pick another card and repeat.
4. Do this three times. See which story you like best.

**b. Try our Desert Island Cards ★**

If you could only take seven cards from the entire deck, I'd take these. Each one helps you with a different stage of your storytelling process. Each card is the "head of a family" of similar tactics.

- *The Dragon & the City*
- *Story Listening*
- *Trust Me, I'm an Expert*
- *Pitch Perfect*
- *Man in a Hole*
- *Movie Time*
- *Story Bank*

**c. Try our Recipe Cards**

Combine different story "ingredients" to solve common problems.
