# Design Methodology

This folder stores your team's **process documentation**: how you run discovery, critiques, sprints, and handoffs.

The copilot checks here for process questions: "How do we run critiques?", "What does our discovery phase look like?", "What's our handoff checklist?"

For interaction theory, visual craft references, and prioritization frameworks, see [`../foundations/`](../foundations/).

---

## What Belongs Here

- **Process guides:** How your team runs sprints, critiques, discovery, or handoffs
- **Facilitation playbooks:** Templates for design sprints, workshops, or research sessions
- **Team agreements:** Working norms, design review SLAs, critique rules
