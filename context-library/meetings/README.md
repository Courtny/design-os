# Meeting Notes

Critique session notes, design syncs, stakeholder reviews, and retros.

The copilot checks here to surface past decisions, action items, and recurring themes.

---

## What Belongs Here

- **Design critique notes** — decisions made, feedback given, action items
- **PM design syncs** — alignment updates, scope changes, priority shifts
- **Stakeholder reviews** — executive or cross-functional feedback
- **Retros** — team retrospective notes
- **Workshop outputs** — synthesis or key outputs from facilitated sessions

**Active notes** (from today or this week) go to `outputs/meeting-notes/` first. Move them here once reviewed.

---

## File Naming

`[meeting-type]-[date].md`

Examples:
- `critique-checkout-redesign-2025-03-28.md`
- `design-pm-sync-2025-03-24.md`
- `stakeholder-review-dashboard-2025-03-10.md`
