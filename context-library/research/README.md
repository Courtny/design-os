# Research

Usability studies, discovery interviews, competitive UX analysis, heuristic evaluations, and survey findings.

The copilot checks here when you ask about user behavior, mental models, or existing evidence for a design decision.

---

## What Belongs Here

- **Usability study reports** — moderated or unmoderated session summaries
- **Discovery research** — problem framing, JTBD interviews, jobs-to-be-done maps
- **Competitive UX analysis** — how competitors solve the same problem
- **Heuristic evaluations** — expert reviews of existing flows
- **Survey summaries** — quantitative preference or satisfaction data
- **Synthesis documents** — themes and insights across multiple studies

**Raw session notes or transcripts** can live here or in `outputs/research-synthesis/` before they're processed.

---

## File Naming

`[study-type]-[topic]-[date].md`

Examples:
- `usability-onboarding-2025-03.md`
- `competitive-ux-checkout-2025-01.md`
- `discovery-fleet-monitoring-2024-11.md`

---

## Using Research in Skills

When you run `/ux-brief` or `/design-spec-draft`, the copilot will check this folder for relevant findings. The more specific your research files, the more grounded the output.
